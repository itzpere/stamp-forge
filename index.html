About This Project:

StampForge is an open-source tool for creating 3D models from SVG files, designed for easy 3D printing of custom stamps and similar objects.

Printing Tips:

  • Recommended: Enable ironing on top-most surfaces in your slicer for a smooth finish
  • ⚠️ WARNING: The positioning system is currently unstable.
